Poem Lyrics

Today we are going to go in Srinagar's flight.

Ready to see this beautiful sight.

I came in the plane and sat on my seat

Ready to fly, ready to eat.

When the plane came on the runway

I did not know what to say.

As and when the plane started to fly,

I couldn't believe. Trust me, I don't lie.

I saw down at the world

Feeling like a flying bird.

I love this flight, I love Indigo.

Get a chance to see the world below.

It had been three hours.

Where are we going? Kashmir or Mars?

It is taking so long.

Where are we going? Kashmir or Hong Kong?

I was very angry. It is so time-taking.

When I called the air hostess she told me we were landing.
