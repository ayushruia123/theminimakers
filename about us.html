About Minimakers

Welcome to MiniMakers! We are a passionate team dedicated to teaching kids technology in a fun and engaging way. Our goal is to prepare children for the future by making learning enjoyable and accessible.

Our Mission

At MiniMakers, our mission is to inspire and educate the next generation of tech-savvy creators. We believe in the power of hands-on learning and strive to make complex technology concepts simple and fun for kids.

Our Story

Founded with a love for creativity and technology, MiniMakers started as a small project to teach kids how to use tools like Tinkercad, Scratch, and Gdevelop. Over time, we've expanded to include web development, photography, and more. Our YouTube channel, MiniMakers, offers tutorials that blend education with entertainment, ensuring that learning is always a joyous experience.
